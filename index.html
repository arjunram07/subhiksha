About Us

The Soul
Behind the Dance

SUBHIKSHA ARJUN · FOUNDER & PRINCIPAL GURU

Subhiksha Arjun is a classically trained Kuchipudi artist and passionate dance educator whose work bridges the sacred traditions of South Indian classical dance with the vibrant energy of contemporary movement.

Trained under renowned gurus, Subhiksha brings authentic storytelling, precise technique, and infectious enthusiasm to every student she teaches — whether they're pursuing a lifetime of dance or simply learning one meaningful piece.

Through Nrityiksha (literally, "dance learning" in Sanskrit), she has cultivated a global community of students across India, the US, UK, Australia, and beyond — all connected by their love for movement and her nurturing, detail-oriented teaching style.
